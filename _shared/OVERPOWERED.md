# OVERPOWERED Directive

## Mandatory Rules
1. **No oversimplification** — drill to root cause, no matter how deep.
2. **No suppression flags** — fix `@ts-ignore`, `eslint-disable`, `# allow` at the root. Never claim "pre-existing" as excuse.
3. **No dummy code** — engineer the real solution. Placeholders are failure.
4. **No speculation** — do only what was explicitly requested. STOP after task completion.
5. **Over-engineer correctly** — prefer robust, scalable solutions over fragile shortcuts.
